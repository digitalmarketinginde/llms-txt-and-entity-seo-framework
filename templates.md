# Templates

This folder contains reusable templates for `llms.txt` and `llms-full.txt`.

The purpose of these templates is to make the process faster, more consistent, and easier to adapt for different business models, markets, and content ecosystems.

## How to use these templates

Use them as a starting point, not as a final answer.

Before filling in a template, define:
- the business goal,
- the brand positioning,
- the ICP,
- the main offer,
- the core topics,
- the relevant channels,
- the existing content units,
- the desired AI-search outcome.

Then adapt the template to the real strategy of the business.

## What these templates support

The templates are designed to help with:
- concise AI-facing brand summaries,
- entity clarity,
- priority page selection,
- channel and content mapping,
- structured recommendations for AI search,
- business-aligned `llms.txt` and `llms-full.txt` creation.

## Strategic rule

Do not use a template to force the business into a generic format.

Use the template to reflect the real positioning, real ICP, and real growth priorities of the business.

The stronger the strategy, the stronger the final file.

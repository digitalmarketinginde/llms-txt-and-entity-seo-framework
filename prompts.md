# AI Search Optimization Prompts

This document contains a comprehensive set of strategic prompts for optimizing website visibility in AI search systems. Each prompt serves a specific purpose within the broader workflow.

## 1) Business Strategy Prompt

**Role:** Senior strategic marketing consultant with expertise in AI-search optimization, entity SEO, multichannel marketing, and revenue growth.

**Objective:** Analyze the business context before any `llms.txt` work begins.

**Inputs Required:**
- Brand: [BRAND]
- Website: [WEBSITE]
- Market: [COUNTRY / MARKET]
- Business model: [BUSINESS MODEL]
- Primary offer: [PRIMARY OFFER]
- Secondary offers: [SECONDARY OFFERS]
- ICP: [ICP]
- Revenue goal: [REVENUE GOAL]
- Strategic priority: [STRATEGIC PRIORITY]
- Available channels: [AVAILABLE CHANNELS]
- Core topics: [CORE TOPICS]

**Deliverables:**
1. A clear explanation of the business goal behind AI-search optimization
2. The role of the website in business growth
3. The role of each active channel in authority, discovery, trust, and conversion
4. The most important commercial outcomes AI-search should support
5. The risks of treating `llms.txt` as a technical task without strategic alignment

**Important:**
- Think strategically, not tactically
- Focus on business growth and revenue impact
- Explain what has to be true before technical files can be useful
- If something is missing, state what additional information is needed
